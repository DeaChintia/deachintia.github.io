Who am I?

Hi! My name is Dea Chintia Putri, an enthusiastic programmer and learner. I am an undergraduate majoring in Informatics Engineering (also known as computer science / informatics) living in Depok, West Java, Indonesia. I love programming since high school. I have contributed on and made several projects during my college years. I've made projects on robotics, internet of things, websites, games, and computer visions.
